Home

Welcome to the official website of Tuzidi foundation, dedicated to making a positive impact in the lives of young people. Our mission is to support education, provide essential resources, and offer mentorship to students across various educational levels.

Our Key Focus Areas:

  • Supporting Education: We assist young people in accessing education from high school through to tertiary institutions and higher learning. Our goal is to remove barriers and provide the support needed for academic success.
  • Providing Sanitary Pads: We ensure that girls in primary and high school have access to sanitary pads, promoting health and comfort during their menstrual cycles.
  • Mentorship Program: Our mentorship initiative, known as Preparing Students for Success (PSSS), helps high school students and recent graduates navigate their future careers and educational paths.
